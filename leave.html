🌿 My Story: Why I Had to Leave

🟢 Reasons to Stay

1️⃣ Uncertainties — maybe things will improve. 50/50 🎲

2️⃣ The sacrifice made is so huge to just let go… but to what end? ⚖️

⚖️ Weighing both sides, I strongly believed leaving was better than staying.

🔴 Why I Had to Leave

1️⃣ Lost Respect. I believe once lost, it can't be recovered. 💔

2️⃣ I Value Peace Above All. Kithyoko was promising — but how could I be peaceful? I had to sacrifice peace or business. I chose peace. 🕊️

3️⃣ Love Lost. How could I explain my absence to Clara? It was over 2 months. Masea grew to hate me more. Love couldn’t be recovered. 💔😔

4️⃣ I Have Become Damaged Goods. Nothing to offer to family — economically or socially. 🧍‍♂️💸

5️⃣ I Prefer Being Independent. I believe life is not to be controlled like a switch. 🔌

6️⃣ Patience vs Begging. Very difficult to differentiate the two. Love needs patience, but love should never beg. Where do we draw the line? 🤷‍♂️❤️
